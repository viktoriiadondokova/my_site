How Cmeraldo was open?

Today I will tell you how a flower that has become a “legend flower” came to this world. In fact, five years ago, when I heard about the Smeraldo at the Game Card Academy event, it was found that these were just legends. (I will talk about the connection of the flower with the cards a little later.)

But on one of the summer days of 2013, I received shocking news. I still remember this day. I washed when the phone rang. This was the friend I met at the Playing Cards Event. We attended a lecture together and were fascinated by that flower, so we kept in touch afterwards. I lived in North Dakota, and she lived in San Francisco, but the distance didn't matter when we had similar hobbies. Returning to the topic, she told me that it seems that the Smeraldo was discovered in real life. I could not say anything for a while. We enthusiastically shouted at each other's handset. The mentioned photograph was the first evidence of the existence of Smeraldo. I still feel warm looking at her.

At this time, a man under the nickname ‘amtest (amare_0)’ was a famous traveler on Instagram, where he published a photo of a flower that he accidentally discovered in northern Italy. ‘Amtest (amare_0)’ had not heard of the Smeraldo before, but understood that it was the most beautiful and unique flower he encountered while traveling. But some people in the comments noticed a similarity to the Smeraldo, so the story spread like a forest fire. Local historians have revealed important information about the old name of this place - ‘La Città di smeraldo’. ‘La Città di smeraldo’ in Italian means “City of Smeraldo”. And, according to historians, in the Middle Ages it was a prosperous village, but after the Black Death pandemic it was abandoned to this day. After that, experts from the Academy of Florists and Biologists from around the world went into the field and publicly confirmed that the flowers are Smeraldo. At that time, it became a huge sensation for Europe and America. Each country tried to grow this flower on its territory, but it was able to grow only in its “Smeraldo City”. There were many reasons for this, but no specifics. Even now, Smeraldo can only grow on farms in northern Italy, and only small quantities can be exported to Europe, America and Asia. You wonder how the flower was able to create such an incredible sensation? I will tell you the story of Smeraldo in the next post.
