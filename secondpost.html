Meet the Smeraldo Flower

Many people believe that flowers are designed for specific, special days. I thought the same way before I became a florist. But the teacher who taught me in America said: “We don’t need flowers on special days, it is flowers that make days like that.”

When I decided to bring Smeraldo to Korea, these words became my strength. "Even if they do not know about it, I hope they will be happy to receive the Smeraldo ..." With these thoughts, I decided to open a special Smeraldo Flowers store in September this year. The interior of Smeraldo Flowers will be in a minimalist style, in which white prevails to emphasize the beauty of the Smeraldo.

Fresh bouquets will be placed on the window differently, depending on the atmosphere of each day, in order to become a fleeting piece of happiness for a passerby. (Am I right? ..) The store is still preparing for opening, but, if necessary, I offer delivery to some areas. But please understand that then they will be in limited quantities. Smeraldo can reflect a different atmosphere, so it is important that you tell me in advance what you want.

Immediate flower delivery is not possible, on average, it takes one week, and if delivery and search are necessary, you should leave the order two weeks before receipt. (Of course, I will try to find everything I need by the deadline.) Every day the weather becomes incredibly stuffy. Since Smeraldo is grown on farms in northern Italy, the abrupt climate change that can occur when transported to Korea will harm the flower. Therefore, delivery will be carried out only within a certain zone, so as not to spoil the appearance. In this way, the Smeraldo can remain fresh and beautiful.
