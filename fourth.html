Legend about Smeraldo flower

Do you think you are beautiful? I once saw an experiment where people had to choose between the doors with the words "I am beautiful" and "I am ugly." If you participated in it, which one would you choose? The reason I remembered this was the story of Smeraldo, of love.

The history of Smeraldo began in a village in northern Italy somewhere in the 15-16 centuries. In a village called ‘La Città di smeraldo’ there was a small secluded castle. An ugly man lived in this castle. Little is known about this man. “He was the duke’s favorite child from a powerful family in the Pyrenees. The duke fell in love with the gardener's daughter and they had a baby, but her mother died of bleeding during childbirth. The duke’s wife and children wanted to get rid of the child, so he helped him escape. ” There were many rumors, but not one was confirmed.

The man hid in the castle, alone. Perhaps due to the fact that from childhood he was faced with hatred and insults, he could not open his heart to anyone. And now he was angry and hiding when someone tried to approach him. His only delight was growing the garden. But once, a woman appeared near the castle. A woman in rags took off her shoes, jumped over the fence and stole a flower. The man was very angry and at first guarded the garden at night. But, catching a moment of slumber, the woman again sneaked into the garden and stole a flower. This went on for several days, until the man pretended to be asleep and tracked how the woman appeared. He was interested. Unconsciously, a man was waiting for her and once followed her.

He covered himself with a cloak, followed her and found that that poor and weak woman was selling flowers in order to survive. The man wanted to help the woman. He wanted to teach her every way to grow flowers that he knew. He wanted to teach her beautiful flowers to grow. But he could not approach the woman. She might have been afraid of him; she would not have liked his ugliness. In the end, all he could do was grow flowers so she kept going to his garden. Man decided to create a flower not from this world. He began to create a flower that a woman could sell at a higher price. A man locked himself in a lock to make a flower. After many countless attempts, he managed to create a flower that never existed in this world and fill it with a garden. But the woman did not appear again. No matter how long he waited, she did not come into the garden. The man became worried and went to the village, covering his face. But the woman was already dead. It was a Smeraldo story. No one knows if she is true, but every time I see a Smeraldo, she comes to my mind and makes me think. What if a man gathered all his courage and showed his face, revealing the truth? A woman could get scared and run away or get angry. Courage is a difficult thing.
