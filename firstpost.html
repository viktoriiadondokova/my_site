Prologue: Fatal encounter with Smeraldo

Hi, I'm a florist Testesso.

The reason I created this blog is because I wanted to tell you about a flower like Smeraldo. At the moment I am preparing for the opening of a store specializing in this flower by mid-September. Someone might think that a blog is a way to promote a store, but I think that my meeting with Smeraldo is not just a business. The thread of fate that connects me with Smeraldo first manifests itself five years ago. I studied abroad in North Dakota (Central North America) and decided. Travel to Virginia to participate in FLOWER 2012, which was organized by the American Florist Academy.

According to the plan, it took five hours to get from North Dakota to Virginia, but it took me seven hours to get from my hostel to the academy. But after this long journey, all that I found at the venue was a banner that says “Origin playing cards. " "FLOWER 2012" ended last week, and then the American Academy of Playing Cards held its event here. I wrote the wrong date in my notebook. At that moment I was excited and dumbfounded, but now, looking back, I think it was fate. I was so tired that I still had no strength to go somewhere else, so I just stayed there to listen to a lecture.

Fortunately, the lecture turned out to be interesting and I was especially fascinated by the part about the flower, because I work as a florist. You guess that this flower was "Smeraldo"? If you look at the photo, you will understand that Smeraldo has its own pleasant atmosphere. Romantic petal shape with a soulful and mysterious color. Don't you feel that a sad story is behind it? And it is true. Smeraldo is a flower with a heartbreaking story. You can familiarize yourself with this story using the language of flowers, in which “non potevo dire la verità” means “truth that cannot be said” translated from Italian. Thinking about this meaning, do you understand that this is a story about love, tragic love? Right? From now on, I will little by little tell in this blog a story about Smeraldo and playing cards.
